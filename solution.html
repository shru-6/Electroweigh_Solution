Who

we are

We are India's largest hydraulic baling press machine manufacturer. In the last 43 years, Advance Hydrau Tech has built a legacy of performance, innovation, quality, and service to become one of the world’s leading providers of efficient and innovative waste management and recycling solutions. Having built products for clients across 90 countries, we have earned recognition for our superior engineering, equipment innovation, and durability. Our machines incorporate latest technology, offer quick ROI, and are custom built to suit our client’s specific requirements.
None of this would be possible without our dynamic and empowering culture. It’s built on a set of shared values that shapes everything we do, both as individuals and as a whole. We sincerely believe in channelling our every effort towards helping you optimize production, improve operational efficiency, and increase profitability.

What

We Do

Advance Hydrau Tech has won accolades for the design and manufacture of state-of-the-art machines and solutions for processing multiple types of metal scraps and wastes. We are the leading manufacturers of hydraulic scrap baling machines, shredders, shears, and other waste management machines. Whether it is scrap metals (ferrous and non-ferrous), automobile scrap, plastic waste, paper waste or agricultural waste, we cater solutions for all. Our experience spanning four decades enables us to provide counsel regarding setting up recycling plants for scrap management and improving the operational efficiency of the existing setup.
